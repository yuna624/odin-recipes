No-Churn Matcha Ice Cream

Description:

Summer's pause preserved.

No churn, no haste‚Äîjust cream and matcha embracing in the cold. Each scoop holds the paradox of matcha: vibrant yet tranquil, rich yet light. A dessert that asks only for patience as it transforms in the freezer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (480ml) heavy cream, cold.
  • 1 can (14oz/400g) sweetened condensed milk.
  • 2 tbsp ceremonial-grade matcha powder (sifted).
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract.
  • Pinch of salt.

Instructions:

  1. Whip cream: Beat cold heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Mix matcha: In another bowl, whisk matcha, condensed milk, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
  3. Combine: Gently fold whipped cream into matcha mixture.
  4. Freeze: Transfer to a loaf pan, cover, and freeze for 6+ hours.

  • Smooth texture: Let ice cream sit at room temp for 5 mins before scooping.
